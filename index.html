The Peaberrys Philosophy

Baking Becomes Art,
Coffee Meets Craft

Born from a love for traditional European bakeries and the slow, expressive coffee cultures of Italy, Peaberrys is a sanctuary of sensory delights. Located in Kolkata's scenic Baranagar district overlooking the serene lake, we invite you to pause, breathe, and savor moments crafted with intent.

Every morning begins with the aroma of freshly baked sourdough, the precise grind of single-origin specialty beans, and pizzas stretched by hand using ancient Neapolitan methods. To us, food is not just nourishment—it is a catalog of shared memories.

The Artisan Bakery
Experience

Step into our bakery corner and smell the comforting aroma of raw flour, warm butter, and crusting breads. Our master bakers utilize slow-fermenting levain and pure ingredients to offer authentic European delicacies.

From the crisp crackle of sourdough to hand-rolled butter croissants and premium bean-to-bar local chocolates, every item is crafted with patience and detail.
